Salvage what is hard to access.

salvaged.work is an archive for texts, audio, and images that have languished within the confines of analogue media. The archive pays homage to these works through curation and digital restoration so that they may be shared with a wider, modern audience.

This project is independently supported and has no commercial interest in the materials shared.
